RULE & HOW TO PLAY

×

- The game has two players playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls two dices once which is added to the player's current score by clicking 'ROLL DICE'

.

- When player rolls the dice and he didn't roll a 1, player clickes on 'HOLD' to add to his total score and it's the next player's turn

- if a player rolls a 1, the player losses his current score, then automatically the next player turn to roll

- if a player rolls a double 1, the player losses his current score and his total score (GLOBAL), then automatically the next player turn to roll

- The first player to reach a 100 point wins by default

- Players can reset the FINAL SCORE to be the default winning score
